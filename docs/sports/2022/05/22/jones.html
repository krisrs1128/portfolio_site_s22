Kevin De Bruyne Shooting

Author: Cameron Jones

Click here to visit the interactive app.

This Shiny app allows the user to visualize and interact with data for all shots taken by soccer player Kevin DeBruyne over the years 2014-2021. The shots can be analyzed by type, location, or expected goal value, a common metric encoding the likelihood of a shot to score.
