NBA Shooting Analysis

Author: Zhi Zheng

Click here to visit the interactive app.

This project implements visualizations with interactions for users to learn and discover the basic summary statistics and interesting trends and habits about 3-point shooters in NBA for 2020 season. There are 3 major tabs. The second tab visualizes all the statistics for all players while the third tab aims to visualize a specific player. Hope you enjoy the app!
