Video Game Sales Data

Author: David Young

Click here to visit the interactive app.

My goal with this app was to determine how much user and critic scores differed and look at which publishers/ developers create high quality games from a users’ (gamers’) perspective. I was also interested in which developers worked with any given publisher and how many copies of their game they were able to sell and quantify how successful an individual game was.
